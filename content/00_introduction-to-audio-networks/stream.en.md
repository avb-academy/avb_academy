A {{< termbase "Stream">}} is a container for data. In {{< termbase "networked audio">}}, this typically means that {{< termbase "Audio Data">}} is transmitted from a source to a sink. {{< termbase "Streams">}} are categorized according to the type of data they carry, for example {{< termbase "Audio Stream">}} for media transport or {{< termbase "Clock">}} Streams for synchronization.
